My research explores how 6G communication, Edge AI, and UAV-based smart surveillance can converge to create real-time, highly efficient, and deployable intelligent systems.

I aim to design frameworks that bring computation closer to the edge, enabling ultra-low latency decisions where cloud dependence is not always feasible.

I work at the intersection of computer vision, wireless architectures, and federated learning. Rather than building only theoretical models, I am driven to create scalable solutions that can operate reliably in actual field scenarios.
